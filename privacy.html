Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CrushTheCompetition ("we," "us," "our") captures, uses, shares, and retains information when you play the game CrushTheCompetition (the "Game"). By using the Game's online features, you consent to the practices described below.

1. How We Capture Data

We capture data in the following ways:

  • Account authentication: When you log in, Epic Online Services (EOS) provides us with a unique account identifier (Account ID / Product User ID) and your display name. This is captured automatically when you authenticate through Epic.
  • Networking data: When you host or join an online session, we automatically capture connection information (such as your network/peer address and session membership) needed to establish gameplay between players.
  • Gameplay and voice data: If you use in-game voice chat, audio is captured from your microphone and transmitted in real time through EOS to other players in your session.

2. How We Use Data

We use the captured data solely to operate the Game's features, including:

  • Authenticating your identity and signing you in to online services
  • Creating, finding, and joining multiplayer sessions (matchmaking)
  • Maintaining real-time network connections between players
  • Enabling voice communication during multiplayer sessions
  • Displaying player names to others in the same session

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information.

3. How We Share Data

We share data only as necessary to provide online play:

  • With Epic Games: Authentication, sessions, and voice chat are processed through Epic Online Services. Your use of these features is also governed by Epic Games' Privacy Policy (epicgames.com/site/privacypolicy).
  • With other players: Your display name and in-session connection/voice data are shared with other players in the same multiplayer session so that gameplay can function.

We do not share your data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

4. How We Retain Data

Connection, session, and voice data is transient: it exists only for the duration of an active multiplayer session and is not stored on our servers after the session ends. We do not maintain our own database of personal information. Account identifiers handled by Epic Online Services are retained by Epic in accordance with Epic's own data retention policies.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly capture, use, share, or retain personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will remove it.

6. Your Choices

You may stop all data capture by not using the Game's online features. Microphone/voice data is only captured when voice chat is enabled.

7. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@supergamerguys.com.
